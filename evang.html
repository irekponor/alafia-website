Evangelism Ministry

At the very core of Alafia Baptist Church's beliefs and mission lies the Evangelism Ministry, a dedicated group driven by the Gospel's mandate to "go and make disciples of all nations." Our purpose is not just to share the message of Christ but to ignite a flame of faith that burns brightly within every heart we touch.

Our Evangelism Ministry understands that the Gospel's message is timeless, yet the methods of delivery must resonate with today's generation. Therefore, we employ a range of approaches, from traditional outreach programs to innovative digital platforms, ensuring that we reach every soul eager for spiritual nourishment, Our Evangelism Ministry understands that the Gospel's message is timeless, yet the methods of delivery must resonate with today's generation. Therefore, we employ a range of approaches, from traditional outreach programs to innovative digital platforms, ensuring that we reach every soul eager for spiritual nourishment, Members of our ministry are equipped with training sessions, workshops, and resources to share Christ's love effectively and compassionately. We focus not just on preaching but also on listening, understanding that every individual's journey to faith is unique and requires a tailored touch, Furthermore, our outreach isn't limited to our local community. Recognizing the universal appeal of Christ's teachings, we organize mission trips, both domestically and internationally, taking the message of salvation to every corner of the globe, However, evangelism at Alafia Baptist Church is not a sole endeavor of a single ministry. We believe that every member of our church family is an evangelist at heart. Through personal testimonies, community engagements, and daily interactions, each of us plays a pivotal role in expanding God's kingdom.

Join the Evangelism Ministry at Alafia Baptist Church and be part of this divine mission, where every effort, no matter how small, leaves an eternal imprint on a soul, drawing them closer to our Savior's embrace.
